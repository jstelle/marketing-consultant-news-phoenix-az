What Are the Top Paying Industries for Marketing Consultants?

Marketing consultants help businesses develop and execute marketing strategies. These professionals are responsible for creating and implementing marketing plans, as well as overseeing other aspects of marketing. Marketing consultants can work in a variety of industries, but some of the best-paying ones include technology, healthcare, and professional services.According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), marketing consultants earned an average annual salary of $62,890 in 2018. If you’re looking to make a career change and enter this field, here’s a breakdown of the top industries for marketing consultants:

Technology

This industry pays well because it’s always looking for people with the right skills to help it grow. For example, if you have experience in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science, you could be an excellent fit for a data science position with one of the tech companies like Google or Amazon.If you’re not familiar with these fields, don’t worry. There are plenty of entry-level positions that don’t require a computer science degree but instead offer training programs.If you’re looking to break into the technology industry, you should know that there are many job opportunities available, but they’re highly competitive. You’ll have to compete with people from around the world for these positions, so it’s important to have a resume that stands out.

Healthcare

The healthcare industry is always looking for qualified individuals to join their team and help them grow. This industry offers many career paths, including marketing, sales, and public relations.If you have a degree in healthcare, you can specialize in marketing and become a marketing manager. Marketing managers oversee the marketing department and develop strategies to promote the brand and increase revenue. They also oversee the communication and public relations teams.If you don’t have a degree in healthcare but are interested in joining the team, you can become a marketing coordinator. A marketing coordinator is responsible for managing the day-to-day marketing activities, such as creating advertisements and marketing materials, managing the budget, and reporting on marketing metrics.Marketing consultants in this industry earn an average salary of $62,000 per year.

Professional Services

This industry is made up of a wide range of services, including consulting, engineering, architecture, and healthcare.Marketing consultants in this industry earn an average salary of $62,000 per year. If you want to break into this industry, you should have a bachelor’s degree in marketing, business, or a related field. If you don’t have a degree, you can attend a post-graduate program to get the necessary skills.

Education

This industry is made up of colleges, universities, and other educational institutions.Marketing consultants in this industry earn an average salary of $62,000 per year. If you want to break into this industry, you should have a bachelor’s degree in marketing, business, or a related field. If you don’t have a degree, you can attend a post-graduate program to get the necessary skills.
