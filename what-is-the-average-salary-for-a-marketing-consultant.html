The average salary for a marketing consultant is $62,000 per year. Marketing consultants may find opportunities in both public and private organizations. They help design, implement, and evaluate marketing strategies that help organizations reach their goals and grow their business. Marketing consultants may work for companies or be self-employed. They may work as part of a team or independently, depending on the organization and their skills. Marketing consultants may also work as freelancers or contractors through marketing agencies. This will depend on the type of work they are hired to do. If working as a contractor, you may have to pay self-employment taxes and report any income from work on a 1099 at the end of the year.
